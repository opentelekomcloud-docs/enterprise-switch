Viewing Metrics
===============

Scenarios
---------

This section describes how to view metrics for an enterprise switch.

Procedure
---------

#. Log in to the management console.

#. Click |image1| in the upper left corner and select the desired region and project.

#. In the upper left corner of the page, click |image2| to open the service list and choose **Management & Deployment** > **Cloud Eye**.

#. In the navigation pane on the left, choose **Cloud Service Monitoring** > **Enterprise Switch**.

#. Locate the enterprise switch and click **View Metric** in the **Operation** column.

   The metrics are displayed.

#. View the metrics.
